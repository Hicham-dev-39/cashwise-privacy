CashWise Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect account data (email, Google Sign-In), device information, usage analytics, and financial records you enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data is used to provide secure authentication, manage subscriptions, improve app performance, and communicate important updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising. Data is shared only with trusted providers (Firebase, Google Play Billing) for authentication and payment processing.

4. Data Security

All sensitive information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard safeguards to prevent unauthorized access.

5. User Control

You can delete your account and associated data at any time. Subscription details are managed transparently through Google Play.

6. App Info and Performance

CashWise collects technical information to ensure the app runs smoothly and to improve stability. This includes:

  • Crash logs – reports of unexpected errors or failures.
  • Diagnostics – technical details such as device type, operating system, and error codes.
  • Other app performance data – information about load times, subscription flow errors, and notification delivery success.

This data is collected automatically through Firebase services and similar tools. It is used only to maintain and improve the app’s performance and is not used to identify individual users.

7. Device or Other IDs

CashWise may collect device or other identifiers automatically through Firebase and Google Play services. These identifiers include unique user IDs, installation IDs, and purchase tokens. They are used solely to provide authentication, manage subscriptions, and improve app performance. CashWise does not use these identifiers to track users across apps or for advertising purposes.

8. App Activity

CashWise collects limited information about how you interact with the app, such as subscription upgrades, badge achievements, and notification actions. This data is required to provide premium features, ensure entitlement logic, and improve user experience. It is also used for analytics and fraud prevention. CashWise does not share this data with third parties and does not use it for advertising or personalization.

9. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Upon request, CashWise will remove your account information, subscription records, and identifiers from Firebase Authentication and Google Play Billing, subject to compliance with legal and financial obligations.
